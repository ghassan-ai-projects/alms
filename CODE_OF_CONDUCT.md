# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and constructive collaboration.

Participants should:

- focus on technical substance
- assume good intent unless evidence shows otherwise
- critique ideas without attacking people
- keep feedback actionable
- avoid harassment, intimidation, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- targeted harassment
- discrimination
- doxxing or exposing private information
- abusive language
- bad-faith disruption of discussion or review

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers rather than escalating them in public discussion.
